Required Reading List

This is a collection of CS-related things that are either often referenced around the halls of West Village H, or otherwise amusing tidbits.

For those who feel that this list is incomplete, this hot mess is on Github.

  • The Acknowledgements, from Olin Shivers. Olin is on sabbatical this year, but he typically teaches a Fundies 1 section.
  • Actually, I'll also recommend the rest of Olin Shivers' website. Personal favorite being The Opinion Files.
  • The Linus Torvald Wikiquote
  • A Brief, Incomplete, and Mostly Wrong History of Programming Languages
  • Jargon Files I'm working on writing a CCIS-flavored one. I think.
  • Kids, it's time you learn what the word "hack" means.
  • Commit Logs from Last Night. You are not alone.
  • The Care and Feeding of Software Engineers (or, why engineers are grumpy) - not really about programming, but about programmers
